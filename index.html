About Gbero

Gbero is Nigeria’s community‑driven ride‑sharing platform. We connect passengers and car owners to share rides, reduce travel costs and ease congestion. Whether you’re commuting within Lagos or heading to another city, Gbero makes it easy to find trusted drivers and fellow travellers. Our platform emphasises safety, convenience and affordability with secure wallets, flexible trip options and real‑time chat.
